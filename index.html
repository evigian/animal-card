Horse

Horses and humans interact in a wide variety of activities such as agriculture, entertainment, and therapy.

  • Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Average Heigth: 142 to 183 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 25 to 30 years
  • Habitat: Wide open spaces with grass

Studies have indicated that horses perform a number of cognitive tasks on a daily basis, meeting mental challenges that include food procurement and identification of individuals within a social system.
